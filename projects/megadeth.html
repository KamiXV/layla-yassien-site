Megadeth

Poster Design

2024
Photoshop

A conceptual poster created by compositing and blending visual elements from scratch, with careful color correction to achieve a cohesive look.

The concept imagines what a concert poster would look like if the metal band Megadeth performed at Bilgi University, combining the band's bold identity with the university.
