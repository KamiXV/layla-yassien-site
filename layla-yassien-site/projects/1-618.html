An academic conceptual project proposing an original video game built around a rich narrative and immersive world. The game explores the idea that our world is shaped and controlled by ideologies and social norms—our own version of "the Matrix."

The proposal covers the entire development process, from the problem statement and research to concept development, target audience, medium, user testing, budget planning, and visual references, creating a complete foundation for the game's universe.
