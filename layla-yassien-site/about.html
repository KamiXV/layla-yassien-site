About Me

My name is Layla Yassien, a Visual Communication
Design graduate from Bilgi University with a one year
minor in Filmmaking.

I'm a flexible and curious designer who enjoys
learning new skills and exploring different creative
fields. I love creativity in all its forms and always look
for new ways to turn ideas into meaningful visual
experiences.

Main Focus

Conceptual Design Art Direction Brand Design Editorial Print Design

Software

Adobe Photoshop Adobe Illustrator Adobe Premiere Pro Adobe After Effects Blender (Beginner) Figma

Interests

I'm very interested in working in the future in Installation Art, Experiential Design, Exhibition Design, or Scenography and anything related to creating immersive experiences.

I'm also passionate about videography and filmmaking, especially music videos.

Another interest of mine is making music. I'm currently learning music theory and playing the electric guitar, with the goal of creating my own music in the future. I enjoy exploring different creative fields whenever I can.

Future Goals

After gaining more industry experience, I hope to grow from a designer into a Creative Director, leading projects from start to finish, shaping creative concepts, and guiding teams through the design process.
